HTML Entities

There are three reserved characters in HTML: < > and &. You should always use HTML entities for these three characters.

Entities always begin with an ampersand (&) and end with a semicolon (;). In between you put in the special code that your browser will interpret as a symbol. In this case, it interprets lt, gt,and amp as less-than, greater-than, and ampersand symbols.
